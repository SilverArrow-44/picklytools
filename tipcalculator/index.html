Tip and split a bill in seconds

Enter the bill, tap a tip preset (or type your own percentage), and set how many people are sharing the cost. Pickly instantly shows the tip amount, the grand total, and exactly what each person owes — no mental math at the table.

How much to tip

In the US, 15–20% of the pre-tax bill is standard for table service, and 20% or more for great service. Counter service and takeaway are often tipped less or not at all. Tipping customs vary widely by country, so adjust the percentage to match where you are.

Rounding for easy splitting

Use the rounding option to round the total up to the nearest dollar, or round each person’s share up so the group comfortably covers the tip — handy when everyone is paying cash.

How much should I tip?
In the US, 15–20% of the pre-tax bill is standard for sit-down service, with 20%+ for great service. Counter service is often tipped less. This calculator lets you set any percentage and splits it across your group.
Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax total?
Tipping on the pre-tax amount is common and perfectly acceptable. Enter your pre-tax bill for a pre-tax tip, or enter the post-tax total if you prefer to tip on that.
How do I split a bill with a tip?
Enter the bill, choose a tip percentage, and set the number of people. The calculator shows the tip, the grand total, and the amount each person owes.

Yes — completely free, no sign-up, and it works on any device.
