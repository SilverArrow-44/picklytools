A free random number generator that's actually fun

Whether you're running a raffle, picking a lucky winner, settling a debate, playing a board game without dice, or teaching probability, this random number generator has you covered. Pick any range, choose how many numbers you want, decide whether repeats are allowed, and get a genuinely random result with a satisfying reveal.

Three modes

  • Single number — one random number between your chosen minimum and maximum.
  • Draw several — pull a set of numbers at once, with the option of no repeats. Perfect for raffles, lottery-style draws, or picking multiple winners.
  • Dice — roll 1–6 dice with classic pip faces and a total. Great when you've lost the dice from your board game.

Is it truly random?

Yes. Results use your browser's cryptographically-strong randomness where available, giving every number in your range an equal chance. Each draw is independent and unbiased.

Common uses

Raffles and giveaways, choosing lottery numbers, picking a winner from numbered entries, random sampling, classroom activities, board and party games, decision-making, and contests on social media.

Frequently asked questions

Can I generate numbers without repeats?
Yes — in "Draw several" mode, tick "No repeats" to get a set of unique numbers, ideal for raffles and draws.
What range can I use?
Any whole-number range you like, including negative numbers, as long as the minimum is below the maximum.
Is it random enough for a raffle?
Yes. It uses your browser's secure randomness, giving each number an equal, independent chance — fair for giveaways and contests.
