About Pickly

Pickly was born from a simple, universal problem: making small decisions is surprisingly hard. Where should we eat? Who goes first? What movie tonight? Which name wins the giveaway? These tiny choices pile up, and the back-and-forth wastes time and energy.

So we set out to build the friendliest, fastest collection of decision-making tools on the web. Each Pickly tool is designed to do one job brilliantly — open it, use it, and get on with your day. No sign-up screens, no cluttered menus, no learning curve.

Who uses Pickly?

Teachers use our spinner as a random name picker to call on students fairly. Streamers and creators run giveaways live on camera. Friends and families settle the eternal "what's for dinner" debate. Managers randomize who speaks first in stand-ups. Event hosts draw raffle winners in front of a crowd. Whatever the decision, there's a Pickly tool to make it quick, fair, and a little bit fun.

Always free, always growing

Pickly is free to use and supported by unobtrusive ads, which lets us keep building new tools for everyone. We add new utilities regularly based on what people ask for — so if there's a tool you wish existed, tell us and it might be next.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know.

Is Pickly really free?
Yes — completely free. Every tool on Pickly works in your browser with no sign-up, no subscription, and no hidden fees. The site is supported by ads, which keeps it free for everyone.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is needed. You can use any tool the moment you open it. We don't ask for your email, name, or any personal information to use Pickly.
Are the random results actually random?
Yes. Our tools use your browser's built-in randomness so every outcome is independent and unbiased — like flipping a fair coin. Past results never affect future ones, so it's perfectly normal to occasionally see the same result twice in a row.
Does Pickly work on my phone?
Absolutely. Pickly is built mobile-first and works on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops in any modern browser like Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge. You can even add it to your home screen for quick access.
Can I share a tool with my own options?
Yes. In the spinner, for example, you can add your own entries and tap "Share This Wheel" to generate a link. Anyone who opens that link sees your exact list — great for giveaways, classrooms, and group decisions.
Is my data private?
Your entries stay in your own browser and aren't sent to us or stored on our servers. Because there's no login, there's no personal profile to worry about. See our Privacy Policy for full details on cookies and advertising.
Will you add more tools?
Yes — regularly. Pickly is designed as a growing hub. We add new decision tools and calculators based on what people request, so check back often or send us a suggestion.
