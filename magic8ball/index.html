The classic Magic 8 Ball, now online

The Magic 8 Ball has been answering life's big (and small) questions since 1950. This free online version brings the whole experience to your screen — think of a yes-or-no question, give the ball a shake, and watch your fortune float up from the murky blue depths. All 20 of the classic answers are here, from a confident "It is certain" to an ominous "Outlook not so good."

How to use it

  • Think of a yes/no question — type it in the box if you like, or just hold it in your mind.
  • Shake the ball by tapping it or pressing the button.
  • Read your answer as it surfaces, color-coded green for positive, red for negative, and yellow for "ask again later."

The 20 classic answers

The traditional Magic 8 Ball has ten positive answers, five non-committal ones, and five negative ones — the same mix used here, so the odds lean slightly optimistic, just like the original toy.

Frequently asked questions

Is the Magic 8 Ball random?
Yes — each shake picks one of the 20 classic answers at random using your browser's secure randomness. It's for fun, not real fortune-telling!
Do I have to type my question?
No, typing is optional. You can just think of your question and shake. The box is there if you'd like to keep track of what you asked.
What questions work best?
Yes-or-no questions: "Will it rain tomorrow?", "Should I order pizza?", "Does my crush like me?" The ball only speaks in yes/no/maybe.
Is it free?
Completely free, no sign-up, supported by unobtrusive ads. See our Privacy Policy.
