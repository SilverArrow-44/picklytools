Play Rock Paper Scissors online, free

Rock Paper Scissors is the classic hand game that settles arguments, decides who goes first, and kills boredom in seconds. This free online version lets you play against the computer any time — no opponent needed. Choose a single round for a quick decision, or go best-of-3 or best-of-5 for a proper match with score tracking and a winner.

The rules (in case you need them)

  • Rock ✊ crushes scissors.
  • Paper ✋ covers rock.
  • Scissors ✌️ cut paper.
  • Same move on both sides? It's a tie — play it again.

Is the computer fair?

Yes — the computer picks its move completely at random using your browser's secure randomness, with no peeking at your choice. Every round is a genuine 1-in-3, so it's as fair as a real game (maybe fairer — no cheating with a late switch!).

Why we love it

It's the fastest fair way to settle a two-person decision, a great quick game for kids, and weirdly satisfying as a best-of series. Use it to decide chores, who pays, who drives, or just to pass the time.

Frequently asked questions

Does the computer cheat?
No. It chooses rock, paper, or scissors at random before you, with no knowledge of your pick. It's completely fair.
What's best-of-3?
First player to win 2 rounds takes the match. Best-of-5 means first to 3. Ties don't count toward either score — you just replay.
Can I play with a friend?
This version is you vs the computer. For two humans, try our Coin Flip or Spinner to decide who goes first!
Is it free?
Completely free, no sign-up, supported by unobtrusive ads. See our Privacy Policy.
