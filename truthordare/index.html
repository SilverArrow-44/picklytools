The classic party game, online and free

Truth or Dare is the party game everyone knows — and this free online version keeps it stocked with hundreds of fresh prompts so you never run out. Pick a mode to match your crowd, choose Truth or Dare, and the card flips to reveal your challenge. It's perfect for sleepovers, road trips, parties, ice-breakers, and date nights. If you want a lighter warm-up first, try our Would You Rather game — 150+ questions with no pressure.

Three modes for any crowd

  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Family — clean, all-ages truths and dares that are safe for kids and grandparents alike.
  • 🎉 Friends — funnier, bolder, and a little embarrassing — the classic teen-and-up experience.
  • 🌶️ Spicy — flirtier, more daring prompts for adults and couples (18+).

How to play

Gather your group and take turns. On your turn, pick Truth or Dare and tap the matching button — the card flips to reveal your prompt. Do the dare or answer the truth honestly, then pass to the next player and tap "Next player." That's it. No setup, no cards to lose, endless prompts.

Truth or Dare questions to get you started

Want a taste before you play? Here are some favourite truth questions and dare ideas from the game — there are hundreds more inside, shuffled fresh every round.

Good truth questions

  • What's the funniest thing that's ever happened to you?
  • If you could have any superpower, what would it be?
  • Have you ever lied to get out of plans?
  • What's a talent you wish you had?
  • Who is your role model and why?
  • What's the best gift you've ever received?
  • If you could visit anywhere in the world, where would you go?
  • What's your most embarrassing childhood memory?

Fun dare ideas

  • Do your best animal impression.
  • Sing the chorus of your favourite song.
  • Talk in an accent for the next 3 rounds.
  • Make up a short rap about the person on your left.
  • Speak in rhymes until your next turn.
  • Walk like a penguin to the door and back.
  • Pretend to be a news reporter for 30 seconds.
  • Tell a joke and make someone laugh.

Whether you call it Truth or Dare, truth and dare, or do-or-dare — it's the same beloved game, and this online version means you never need a question list or printed cards again.

Frequently asked questions

Is it suitable for kids?
Yes — use Family mode for clean, all-ages prompts. Friends and Spicy modes are for older players; Spicy is intended for adults (18+).
How many prompts are there?
Hundreds across the three modes, shuffled so you won't repeat one until you've worked through the set.
Can we play in a big group?
Definitely — just pass the device around or put it on a screen. There's no player limit.
Is it free?
Completely free, no sign-up, supported by unobtrusive ads. See our Privacy Policy.
