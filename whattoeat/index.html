Can't decide what to eat? Let us pick for you

"What should I eat?" might be the most-asked question of all time — and the hardest to answer when you're hungry and tired. This free food decider takes the agony out of it. Tell it your mood — what meal, which cuisine, how much effort you feel like, and any dietary preference — and it instantly suggests a specific dish. Don't feel like choosing at all? Hit "Surprise me" and let it pick completely at random.

How it works

  • Set your mood (or leave everything on "Any"). Pick a meal, a cuisine, how much effort, and a diet style.
  • Tap the button and watch it roll through options before landing on your meal.
  • Not feeling it? Tap "Try again" for another idea that still matches your filters, or "Surprise me" to throw the rules out entirely.

Why it helps

Decision fatigue is real, and food is one of the most common things we stall on. Handing the choice to a quick, fun randomizer removes the back-and-forth — especially useful for couples and groups who can never agree, busy people who just want an answer, or anyone stuck in a mealtime rut and looking to try something new.

Frequently asked questions

How does it choose a meal?
It filters a large list of dishes by your selections, then picks one at random from the matches. Each tap gives a fresh suggestion.
What if nothing matches my filters?
If your combination is too narrow, it lets you know and suggests loosening a filter — or you can hit "Surprise me" for a totally random pick.
Can it suggest vegetarian or healthy options?
Yes — use the Diet filter for vegetarian, healthy, or comfort-food picks.
Is it free?
Completely free, no sign-up, supported by unobtrusive ads. See our Privacy Policy.
