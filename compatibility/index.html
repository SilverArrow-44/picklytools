How the compatibility score works (and a fair warning)

First, the important part: this is a game, not science. The Compatibility Calculator is built purely for fun — to spark a laugh between friends, break the ice at a party, or settle a playful “are we a match?” debate. It is not a real measure of any relationship.

What goes into the score

The calculator blends the two names and birthdays you enter into a single playful percentage. Because it is based on a fixed formula rather than randomness, the same two names always produce the same score — so you cannot just keep re-rolling for a better number.

Star signs and the result

Alongside the names, it reads the zodiac signs from your birthdays and factors a light “sign harmony” into the result. Astrology is not science either, but it makes the reveal more fun.

Fun ways to use it

Couples test their “official” match percentage, friends compare who pairs best, and party hosts use it as an icebreaker. Pair it with Would You Rather or Truth or Dare for a full game night.

How is the compatibility score calculated?
The score uses a deterministic algorithm based on the numerical values of each name's characters. If birthdays are added, star sign compatibility is factored in too. It's purely for entertainment.
Is this compatibility calculator accurate?
No — and it's not meant to be! This is a fun party game tool. Real compatibility is built over time through shared values, communication, and experiences. The algorithm always gives the same pair the same score, which makes it fun to compare with friends.
Can I use this for any relationship?
Yes — friends, partners, colleagues, even rivals. It works for any two names you're curious about.
Does adding birthdays change the score?
Yes — when you add both birthdays, the calculator includes a birthday compatibility element and shows your star signs alongside the score.
What does 99% compatibility mean?
It means the universe approves of this pairing — according to our very scientific name-hash algorithm 😄. Take it as a good omen and have fun with it.
