Find a meeting time that works for everyone

Choose two or three time zones and your preferred working hours, and Pickly highlights the block of time that falls inside everyone’s working day — shown in each person’s local time so there’s no mental conversion.

When there’s no overlap

Some zones are simply too far apart for a standard 9-to-5 overlap — the US West Coast and India, for example. Widen the working hours, or accept an early/late call for one side. The planner tells you immediately when no shared window exists.

A note on daylight saving

This tool uses standard UTC offsets and does not automatically adjust for daylight saving time, so double-check your window around DST changeovers in spring and autumn.
