Never Have I Ever, online and endless

Never Have I Ever is the confession game that reveals everyone's secrets one prompt at a time. This free online version serves up hundreds of statements across clean, party, and spicy modes — just tap for the next one. It's a perfect ice-breaker and a party staple for groups of any size.

How to play

  • Everyone starts with 10 fingers up (or a drink, if you're playing that way).
  • Read the prompt aloud. If you've done it, put a finger down (or take a sip).
  • Tap Next for a new prompt. Last person with fingers up wins — or just play for the stories.

Three modes

  • 😇 Clean — friendly, all-ages prompts good for family and classrooms.
  • 🎉 Party — bolder and funnier for friends and teens-and-up.
  • 🌶️ Spicy — daring, flirtier prompts for adults (18+).

Frequently asked questions

How do you play Never Have I Ever?
Someone reads a "Never have I ever…" statement. Anyone who HAS done it puts a finger down or takes a sip. Last person with fingers remaining wins.
Is it family-friendly?
Use Clean mode for all-ages fun. Party and Spicy are for older players; Spicy is intended for adults (18+).
How many prompts are there?
Hundreds across the three modes, shuffled so they don't repeat until you've gone through the set.
Is it free?
Completely free, no sign-up, supported by unobtrusive ads. See our Privacy Policy.
