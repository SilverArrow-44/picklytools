A free slot machine, purely for fun

Get the thrill of spinning reels without spending a cent. This free online slot machine is built purely for entertainment — you start with virtual coins, place a pretend bet, pull the spin, and watch three reels of classic fruit and lucky symbols whirl into place. Match symbols on the payline to win virtual coins, with confetti for the big wins. There's no real money involved, nothing to buy, and no sign-up.

How to play

  • Set your bet with the − and + buttons (it just uses your virtual coins).
  • Tap SPIN and watch the three reels roll.
  • Match symbols on the center line to win — three 7️⃣s is the jackpot.
  • Run low? Hit "Reset coins" to top back up to 100. It's all pretend!

Just for fun — no gambling

This is a toy, not a casino. The coins have no value, can't be bought or cashed out, and there's no way to deposit money. It's the simple, nostalgic fun of pulling a slot lever, minus any risk.
